1)This is a Third Person Perspective Game where player can move in 3D directions using Arrow keys.
2)Player Needs to collect coins in order to finish the level.
3)There Are four types of Enemies try to hit the player
(Three of them are movable while one is Machine Gun fixed positioned).
4)Enemies try to hit the player and Machine Gun Double shoots fire at 90's angle repeatedly.
5)Two types of PowerUps are offered(hidden in the map).
6)Red is to Reset the health(in case it got down)
7)Blue is to Double the number of coins.
then
8)Find Pandora Box in the map and hit it(If you've enough no. of coins then level is completed otherwise you need to collect max coins to complete level).
